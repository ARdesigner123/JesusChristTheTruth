What are the steps to Salvation?

Admin / Owner Posted 2 days ago

Salvation is the greatest gift God offers to humanity. It is not earned through our own good works, but is received entirely by grace through faith. Here are the biblical steps, often referred to as the "Romans Road":


1. Acknowledge our Sin: "For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." (Romans 3:23). We must first realize we need saving.

2. Understand the Consequence: "For the wages of sin is death, but the gift of God is eternal life in Christ Jesus our Lord." (Romans 6:23).

3. Believe in Christ's Sacrifice: "But God demonstrates his own love for us in this: While we were still sinners, Christ died for us." (Romans 5:8).

4. Confess and Surrender: "If you declare with your mouth, 'Jesus is Lord,' and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved." (Romans 10:9).


Real-Life Example: Imagine you are drowning in the middle of the ocean. No matter how hard you swim, you cannot save yourself; you are exhausted. A rescue helicopter drops a lifeline right to you. The lifeline is Jesus. You don't "earn" the rescue by swimming harder; you simply have to grab the rope and trust the rescuer to pull you up. That is faith!
