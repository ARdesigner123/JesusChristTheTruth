Prince of Peace — Painting by Akiane Kramarik

✧ The Painting

“Prince of Peace” is a world-renowned portrait of Jesus Christ painted in 2003 by Akiane Kramarik when she was just eight years old. The artwork is celebrated for its extraordinary realism, emotional depth, and its powerful expression of peace, love, and compassion.

✧ How The Painting Came About

From a very young age, Akiane experienced vivid spiritual visions that inspired her to create art. She spent years searching for a face that matched what she saw in her visions.

Eventually, a traveling carpenter came to her home and became the model for the painting, allowing her to bring her vision of Jesus Christ to life.

✧ Details Of The Artwork

The painting was completed in approximately 40 hours using oil on canvas. It is known for its luminous quality, intricate detail, and lifelike expression.

The original piece was later stolen and remained hidden for nearly 16 years before being recovered. Today, it is displayed at the Belóved Gallery in Texas.

✧ About Akiane Kramarik

Akiane Kramarik is a child prodigy artist and poet who began painting at a very young age. Her works are often inspired by her spiritual experiences and have gained global recognition.

She has created multiple paintings of Jesus Christ, including “Father Forgive Them” and other powerful interpretations of faith and divinity.

“Art is not what you see, but what you make others see.”
