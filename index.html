Jesus Christ The Truth

"For God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish but have eternal life."

— John 3:16 (NIV)

The True Gospel Of Jesus Christ

1. God Is Holy

“Holy, holy, holy is the Lord Almighty; the whole earth is full of His glory.”

— Isaiah 6:3 (NIV)

2. All Have Sinned

“For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God.”

— Romans 3:23 (NIV)

3. The Consequence Of Sin

“For the wages of sin is death, but the gift of God is eternal life in Christ Jesus our Lord.”

— Romans 6:23 (NIV)

4. Christ Died For Us

“But God demonstrates His own love for us in this: While we were still sinners, Christ died for us.”

— Romans 5:8 (NIV)

5. Salvation Through Jesus Alone

“Jesus answered, 'I am the way and the truth and the life. No one comes to the Father except through me.'”

— John 14:6 (NIV)

6. Believe And Be Saved

“If you declare with your mouth, 'Jesus is Lord,' and believe in your heart that God raised Him from the dead, you will be saved.”

— Romans 10:9 (NIV)

7. Grace, Not Works

“For it is by grace you have been saved, through faith—and this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God—not by works, so that no one can boast.”

— Ephesians 2:8-9 (NIV)

8. Repent And Turn To God

“Repent, then, and turn to God, so that your sins may be wiped out, that times of refreshing may come from the Lord.”

— Acts 3:19 (NIV)

9. Faith Without Works Is Dead

“As the body without the spirit is dead, so faith without deeds is dead.”

— James 2:26 (NIV)

10. Christ Rose Again

“He is not here; He has risen, just as He said.”

— Matthew 28:6 (NIV)

11. New Life In Christ

“Therefore, if anyone is in Christ, the new creation has come: The old has gone, the new is here!”

— 2 Corinthians 5:17 (NIV)

12. The Gift Of Eternal Life

“And this is the testimony: God has given us eternal life, and this life is in His Son.”

— 1 John 5:11 (NIV)
